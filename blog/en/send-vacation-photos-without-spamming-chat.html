Send Vacation Photos Without Spamming Chat

Instead of sending 40 photos one by one, put them into one share link and send one message.

Easy setup

  1. Pick your best photos (up to 25 in one link).
  2. Create one share link.
  3. Drop that link in WhatsApp, Messenger, or Facebook chat.
  4. If needed, create a QR from the same link.
  5. Set expiry or disable it later.

Why this feels better

  • Chat stays clean
  • People open one organized set
  • You stay in control after sending
