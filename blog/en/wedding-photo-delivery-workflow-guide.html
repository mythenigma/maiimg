Wedding Photo Delivery Workflow Guide

Wedding delivery is emotional and time-sensitive. Keep access simple for families and controllable for your team.

Simple structure that works

  1. Create focused sets (highlights, ceremony, reception).
  2. Keep each set under 25 images for clean review.
  3. Share with one direct link and one QR for printed cards.
  4. Set open limits/expiry before sending.
  5. Revoke old links after final delivery completes.

Why clients like this flow

They get one clear entry point and no confusion between old and final versions.
