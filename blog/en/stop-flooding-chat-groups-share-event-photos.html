The Etiquette of Event Photo Sharing

Being a good event photographer isn't just about taking great photos - it's about sharing them respectfully:

  • Ask before taking photos of people - not everyone wants to be photographed
  • Don't share unflattering photos - if someone looks bad, just delete it
  • Respect privacy settings - if someone asks you not to share certain photos, honor that
  • Give people time to download - don't expire links too quickly
  • Let others contribute - encourage other people to add their photos to the same gallery
