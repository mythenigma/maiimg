Know Who's Really Viewing Your Files: IP Tracking for Secure Sharing

Sometimes you need to know more than just "someone viewed your file." With optional IP tracking, see exactly where your file views are coming from - perfect for security-conscious sharing and business analytics.

üîç Optional Enhancement, Not Required

IP tracking is an optional feature in MaiIMG. Most users will never need it, but for those who do, it's there when you want that extra layer of insight and security.

Beyond Basic View Counts

Most file sharing services tell you "5 people viewed your file." That's useful, but sometimes you need to know more:

  • Are these views from people you actually shared with?
  • Is someone unauthorized accessing your files?
  • Are your business documents being viewed from expected locations?
  • Did your international client actually review the proposal?

IP tracking gives you these answers without being invasive or complicated.

How It Works (Simple and Optional)

üîß Enable When You Need It

When sharing files, you can optionally enable IP tracking. It's off by default - only turn it on when you specifically want location insights.

When enabled, you'll see:

  • General location: City/region level (not exact addresses)
  • View timestamps: When each view happened
  • Access patterns: Repeated views from the same location
  • Geographic distribution: Where your content is being viewed

üîí Privacy-Conscious Design

IP tracking shows general location data (city/region), not specific addresses or personal information. It's designed for legitimate security and analytics needs, not surveillance.
