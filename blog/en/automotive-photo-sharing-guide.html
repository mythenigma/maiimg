Best Practices for Automotive Photography

1. Basic Photography Techniques

üì∏ Essential Photography Points

  • Good lighting: Choose the best lighting time for shooting, avoid shadows
  • Multiple angles: Shoot cars from various angles to show the full picture
  • Clear details: Ensure car details are clearly visible
  • Accurate colors: Maintain accurate car colors
  • Beautiful composition: Pay attention to photo composition and aesthetics

  • Macro photography: Show fine craftsmanship and materials
