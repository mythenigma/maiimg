Photo Sharing Workflow Comparison for Marketing Teams

Most delays happen after upload. Better workflow means clear packaging, clear channels, and clear closing rules.

Workflow A: Upload-first, then patch

Fast at the start, messy at the end. Teams often manually add rules later and struggle to retire old links.

Workflow B: Scope-first, then publish

Set scope and access before sharing. This keeps campaigns cleaner and reduces confusion across teams.

A practical baseline

  • One link per campaign task, up to 25 images
  • Direct link + QR from the same source URL
  • Set open limits and expiry before launch
  • Revoke once campaign window closes
