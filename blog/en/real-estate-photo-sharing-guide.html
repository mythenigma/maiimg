Best Practices for Property Photography

1. Basic Photography Techniques

üì∏ Essential Photography Points

  • Good lighting: Choose the best lighting time for shooting, avoid shadows
  • Multiple angles: Shoot properties from various angles to show the full picture
  • Clear details: Ensure property details are clearly visible
  • Accurate colors: Maintain accurate property colors
  • Beautiful composition: Pay attention to photo composition and aesthetics

2. Advanced Photography Techniques

  • Wide-angle shooting: Use wide-angle lenses to show spatial sense
  • Panoramic photography: Take panoramic photos of properties
  • Detail close-ups: Show fine craftsmanship and materials
  • Comparison shots: Compare with surrounding properties
  • Emotional expression: Convey property emotional value through photos

3. Post-Processing Techniques

  • Color adjustment: Adjust photo brightness, contrast, and saturation
  • Background processing: Remove or replace backgrounds
  • Detail enhancement: Enhance property detail representation
  • Style consistency: Maintain consistent style across all property photos
  • Size optimization: Optimize photo sizes for different platforms
