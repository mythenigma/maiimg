Share Family Photos with a QR Code

If family members use different apps, QR is the easiest universal option: scan and open.

How to do it

  1. Create one photo set (up to 25 images).
  2. Generate one share link.
  3. Create QR from the same link.
  4. Print or send the QR image in family groups.
  5. Set expiry for temporary events if needed.

Good for

  • Reunions and holiday gatherings
  • Family albums shared across generations
  • Offline handouts and printed cards
