How to Share Photos Safely (Step by Step)

  1. Upload to MaiIMG: Go to MaiIMG.com and upload your photos. No account creation needed!
  2. Set a Strong Password: Choose a password that's easy for you to share with trusted people but hard for others to guess.
  3. Choose Expiration: Decide how long you want the photos to be accessible. A week? A month? You decide.
  4. Disable Downloads (Optional): If you don't want people to save copies, turn on download protection.
  5. Share Responsibly: Send the link and password separately for extra security. Text the link, email the password, for example.

Privacy Tips from Security Experts

  • Never share passwords in the same message as links: Send them through different channels (text vs email)
  • Use descriptive passwords: Instead of "123456", try something like "FamilyVacation2025" that's meaningful to your group
  • Check your sharing analytics: Regularly look at who's accessing your photos and when
  • Delete when done: Remove photos from any platform when you no longer need to share them
  • Trust your instincts: If a platform asks for too much personal information just to share photos, find an alternative
